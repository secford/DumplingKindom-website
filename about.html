About Us

Bringing authentic Chinese comfort food to San Francisco's Sunset District

Our Story

Asian American Foods SF Inc. is a beloved neighborhood restaurant nestled in the heart of San Francisco's Sunset District. We specialize in handmade Chinese dumplings, wontons, buns, and traditional braised dishes that bring the authentic flavors of China to every table.

Every dumpling is carefully crafted by hand using fresh, quality ingredients. Our recipes draw from time-honored Chinese culinary traditions, creating flavors that are both familiar and extraordinary.

With over 40 varieties of dumplings to choose from, alongside crispy pot stickers, fluffy steamed buns, and savory pancakes, we offer something for every palate and dietary preference.

🥗

Handmade Fresh

Every dumpling and wonton is handmade daily using time-tested techniques. No shortcuts, no factory production — just honest, homemade food.

🌿

Quality Ingredients

We source fresh vegetables, premium meats, and the finest ingredients to ensure every bite delivers authentic, vibrant flavors.

❤️

Community First

As part of the Sunset District community, we take pride in serving our neighbors with warmth, care, and the comforting taste of home.

More Than Just Dumplings

While we are famous for our handmade dumplings, our menu extends to a wide array of traditional Chinese comfort foods. From crispy green onion pancakes and savory chive boxes to hearty braised pork belly and tender beef shank, every dish reflects our dedication to authentic Chinese cuisine.

Whether you're picking up a quick meal or stocking your freezer with our frozen dumplings for the week, we are here to make your dining experience both convenient and delicious.
